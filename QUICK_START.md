# DevTables Quick Start Guide

Welcome to **DevTables**! This guide will help you get started with our weekly developer dinners in Medellin.

## What is DevTables?

DevTables brings developers together for small-group dinners every Wednesday. You'll be matched with 5 other developers at a local restaurant, cafe, or coworking space for a 90-minute dinner with guided conversation prompts. It's free to joinâ€”you just split the bill with your table.

**Perfect for:**
- Meeting like-minded developers
- Finding collaborators for projects
- Learning about new technologies
- Practicing English or Spanish
- Building your professional network

### Step 4: Attend the Dinner ðŸŽ‰

#### Before You Go

- **Check your table assignment** - Know your venue and check-in code
- **Arrive on time** - Events start at 7:00 PM sharp
- **Bring your phone** - You'll need it for check-in and icebreakers

#### At the Venue

1. **Check In:**
   - Go to the event's check-in page on your phone
   - Enter your 4-character table code (found on your table or in your dashboard)
   - Confirm your check-in

2. **Meet Your Tablemates:**
   - Once 3+ people check in, icebreakers unlock
   - Go to the icebreakers page on your phone
   - Read the table norms (phones away, listen actively, etc.)

3. **Follow the Icebreakers:**
   - The app will show conversation prompts in 3 rounds:
     - **Round 1 (Warm-up)**: Fun, light questions (~20 min)
     - **Round 2 (Depth)**: Career and learning questions (~40 min)
     - **Round 3 (Future)**: Forward-looking, collaborative questions (~20 min)
   - Click "Next Prompt" to advance through the prompts
   - A 90-minute timer keeps you on track

4. **Last Drinks (Optional):**
   - At 75 minutes, the app reveals an after-party venue
   - You can join other tables there for more networking
   - Starts 30 minutes after dinner ends

âœ… **Done!** You've completed your first DevTables dinner!

## After the Dinner

### Give Feedback & Connect

1. Go to the event's feedback page (you'll see a link after the event)
2. **Rate the event:** Give it 1-5 stars
3. **Rate each tablemate:**
   - **Connect** - You'd like to stay in touch
   - **Neutral** - No preference
   - **Report** - If there was an issue (admin will review)
4. Add optional notes for people you want to connect with
5. Submit your feedback

### Mutual Connections

- If **both** you and a tablemate select "Connect," you'll be matched!
- You'll see a notification: "You matched with [Name]!"
- Full profiles unlock (GitHub, LinkedIn, email) for mutual connections
- View all your connections on the **"Connections"** page

## Frequently Asked Questions

### How much does it cost?
Events are **free to book**. You just split the bill with your tablemates at the venue (typically 15,000-30,000 COP per person).

### How are tables assigned?
Our algorithm matches you with diverse developers based on:
- Tech stack diversity
- Role diversity (frontend, backend, full-stack, etc.)
- Experience level
- Goals and interests
- Language preferences

### Can I choose my tablemates?
No, tables are assigned by our matching algorithm to ensure diversity and fresh connections. You won't be matched with the same people from your last 4 events.

### What if I can't make it?
You can cancel your booking anytime before the event. Just go to the event page and click "Cancel Booking."

### What if I have a problem with someone?
You can report someone during the feedback process. Select "Report" and provide details. Our admin team will review all reports.

### Can I block someone?
Yes, you can block users from your connections page or their profile. Blocked users won't be matched with you in future events.

### What languages are supported?
The app supports both English and Spanish. You can set your preference when creating your profile. The matching algorithm ensures language compatibility at tables.

### How do I update my profile?
Go to your profile page and edit any information. Changes will be reflected in future matchings.
